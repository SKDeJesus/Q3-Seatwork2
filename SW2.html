Sanrio characters Description

Cinnamoroll is a white puppy with long ears that resemble cinnamon rolls, hence the name. The character has a chubby, round face and big blue eyes, adding to its endearing look. Cinnamoroll is often depicted with a blue collar adorned with a small yellow bell.

Kuromi is depicted as a punk-style girl with distinctive features such as a black jester's hat with a pink skull on the front, a black eye patch over her left eye, and a black outfit with a pink skull on her chest. She often carries a magic wand or a scepter. Kuromi's color palette typically includes shades of black, white, and pink.

Pompompurin is a golden retriever dog character characterized by its distinctive brown beret, which is often tilted to the side of its head. The name "Pompompurin" is derived from the English words "pom-pom," referring to the bobble on its beret, and "pudding," as the character has a love for custard puddings.

My Melody is depicted as a white rabbit with pink or red hood-like ear covers that resemble a hood with a bow tied under her left ear. She is often portrayed wearing a red or pink hood and is known for her sweet and gentle personality. Unlike Hello Kitty, My Melody is depicted with a mouth.

Hello Kitty is a fictional character created by the Japanese company Sanrio. She made her debut in 1974 and has since become a globally recognized and iconic character. Hello Kitty is portrayed as a cute and anthropomorphic white cat with a red bow on her left ear. She typically lacks a mouth, which adds to her simple and timeless design.
